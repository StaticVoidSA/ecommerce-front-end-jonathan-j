Why Join Smart Shopper?

As a Smart Shopper you earn points every time you swipe your Smart Shopper card! Points can be spent on anything you like and are just one of the ways we save you money. Another way we reward Smart Shoppers is with personalised discounts every two weeks which are automatically loaded to your Smart Shopper card, as long as we have contact details for you and you have opted in to the Smart Shopper programme. You can use your discounts in-store, and also online when you link your Smart Shopper card to your online profile.

How to claim your Smart Shopper Points and Personalised Discounts

Earn Smart Shopper points with each purchase when you swipe your card or virtually "swipe" with the Pick n Pay mobile app. Your personalised discounts are automatically loaded onto your Smart Shopper card every two weeks, and you can view your discounts on the Pick n Pay mobile app or your biweekly mailer. Make sure that you have added contact info to your Smart Shopper profile for us to be able to automatically load your discounts.
